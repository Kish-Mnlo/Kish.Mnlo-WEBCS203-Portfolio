About Cosmicore..

Cosmicore first started as a project that was created with the intention to create one of the best software and hardware to be used in the near future.

It first started out with a team of 5 filled with hopes and dreams and eventually blossomed into what we call Cosmicore today!

Our mission: Reaching for the stars

We, as innovators, believe in knowing no limits and always reaching for the stars. Cosmicore is a company that is willing to learn new things, create things never seen before, and encourages creativity.

Our vision: Improvement means success

We strive to keep improving our projects to deliver the best quality every person deserves.

How do we operate?

As a company that first started off with a small team, we value teamwork, dedication and commitment when it comes to starting a project. We incorporate these values into our work environment and help those who struggle to. We also want to foster a kind, approachable and open-minded team as we value having creativity when creating different projects.


We also actively engage with our co workers to create a friendly environment and encourage everyone to not be afraid to share their thoughts during a discussion. Overall, we want to create a safe and comfortable area for our workers to work easily and effectively.
